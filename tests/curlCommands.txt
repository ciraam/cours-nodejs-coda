## GET
curl -v http://localhost:3001/api/contacts

### GET/id
curl -v http://localhost:3001/api/contacts/1

## POST
curl -X POST http://localhost:3001/api/contacts \
     -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
     -d '{"id":3, "firstname": "Moine", "lastname": "Test", "email": "encore@test.fr", "address": "Rue de la RedBull"}'

## PUT
curl -X PUT http://localhost:3001/api/contacts/1 \
     -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
     -d '{"firstname": "Moine", "lastname": "Test", "email": "encore@test.fr", "address": "Rue de la RedBull"}'

## DELETE
curl -X DELETE http://localhost:3001/api/contacts/2
